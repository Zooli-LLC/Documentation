Backing up your Podio data is crucial to ensuring that your company's critical information is protected in the event of accidental deletion. Podio does not have a built-in mechanism to recover deleted content, so it is up to each organization to ensure that their data is safely backed up.

We highly recommend using [Momentum Tools Backup & Restore](https://momentumtools.io/?utm_source=zooli) as the simplest way of backing up your Podio workspaces. Backup & Restore will sync your Podio apps to a secured Google Drive folder daily. They also offer a built-in restore function, making it quick and easy to recover data that is deleted. This is a full backup solution that even includes files, comments, and tasks---something that is not offered through Podio's built-in export feature.

If you are not ready for a paid solution, you can also use Podio's [Excel Export](https://docs.sharefile.com/en-us/podio/using-podio/using-apps/exporting-to-excel.html) feature to create regular backups of your Podio apps. This must be done manually for each app. App data that is exported to Excel will **not** include comments, tasks, images, or files attached to the item.

Regardless of the backup solution you choose, it is incredibly important to have a plan in place in the event of data loss---whether via accidental deletion or by a disgruntled employee.
