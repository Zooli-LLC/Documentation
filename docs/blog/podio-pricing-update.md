# Navigating Podio’s New Pricing Changes: What You Need to Know (and What to Do Next)

For years, Podio has been a reliable workhorse for businesses managing complex workflows, largely thanks to its generous Premium tier that included a massive 5 million automation actions per month. However, a recent shift in Podio’s pricing model is leaving many power users with unexpected choices to make. 

Podio is rolling out new limits **effective August 1st**, reducing the included automation actions for Premium users from 5 million down to just 25,000 per month. For organizations that rely heavily on Podio's automation engine to run their daily operations, hitting this new ceiling will happen fast.

Here is a breakdown of the recent changes, what they mean for your bottom line, and the options you have moving forward.

---

### The New Reality: Automation and API Add-ons

If your organization exceeds the new 25,000 monthly action limit when the new pricing goes live on August 1st, you will be required to purchase an Automation Add-on to keep your workflows running. 

Based on the newly released pricing tiers, scaling back up to the capacity you previously enjoyed will come at a premium:

* **100,000 actions/month:** $39/month
* **500,000 actions/month:** $119/month
* **1,000,000 actions/month:** $159/month
* **5,000,000 actions/month:** $559/month

In addition to automation actions, Podio is also introducing tiered API add-ons for businesses with higher integration needs, starting at $23/month for 2,500 calls per hour, and scaling up to $159/month for 50,000 calls per hour. 

For heavy users, maintaining your current infrastructure on Podio could mean a significant increase in your monthly SaaS overhead.
